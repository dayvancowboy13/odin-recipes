Butter Chicken

Description

A delicious dish of Indian origin. Just a tang of spice!

Ingredients:

  • Chopped Chicken Breast
  • Butter chicken sauce (from Costco!)
  • Naan
  • Quinuoa
  • Butter

Directions:

  1. Heat pan to medium heat and add ~15g butter
  2. Chop 180g chicken breast into ~1cm cubes. Add to pan when butter starts to sizzle
  3. Set oven temperature to ~200 deg ceclius. Heat Naan for ~4 mins, then flip and heat until soft
  4. Once chicken is mostly cooked (white color), add butter chicken sauce.
  5. Let cook in pan for 3-5 mins. Then, reduce heat and let simmer for 2 minutes.
  6. Add quinuoa to bowl. Add butter chicken mix from pan.
  7. Enjoy!
