Greek Yogurt with Berries

Description

A hearty breakfast berry blast! A simple recipe combining frozen berries, greek yogurt, and protein powder!

Ingredients:

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Frozen Berries
  • Protein Powder
  • Pumpkin Flax

Directions:

  1. Pour 50g frozen berries and let sit until defrosted
  2. Add 150g greek yogurt
  3. Add 15-20g protein powder and mix
  4. Add 15-20g pumpkin flax
  5. Enjoy!
