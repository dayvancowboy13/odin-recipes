Protein and Oats

Description

A filling morning treat! Provides carbs and protein to get your day started right!

Ingredients:

  • Steel cut oats
  • Peanut Powder
  • Protein Powder
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Xanthum Powder

Directions:

  1. Add 50g oats to bowl
  2. Add 15g peanut powder to bowl
  3. Add 5-10g cocoa powder to bowl
  4. Add 15g protein powder to bowl
  5. Add 150g greek yogurt and 150g water to bowl and mix
  6. Add 5g xanthum powder and mix again
  7. Let sit in refridgerator over night
  8. Enjoy!
